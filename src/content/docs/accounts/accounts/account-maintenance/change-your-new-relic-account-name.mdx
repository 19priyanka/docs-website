Some users are able to update their account settings and preferences. To change your account name:

1. Go to [one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com), click the [account dropdown](/docs/accounts-partnerships/education/getting-started-new-relic/glossary#account-dropdown), and select **Account settings**.
2. Under **Account information**, next to your account name, select the **Edit** <Icon name="edit-2"/>
   icon.
