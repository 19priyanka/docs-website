After you send OpenTelemetry data to New Relic, you can use a variety of UI pages to analyze it. Take a look at these options:

  * [Find entities and data:](/docs/more-integrations/open-source-telemetry-integrations/opentelemetry/opentelemetry-view-your-data/opentelemetry-find-entities) See how to filter your entities and data
  * [Summary:](/docs/more-integrations/open-source-telemetry-integrations/opentelemetry/opentelemetry-view-your-data/opentelemetry-summary-page) Get the big picture
  * [Distributed tracing:](/docs/more-integrations/open-source-telemetry-integrations/opentelemetry/opentelemetry-view-your-data/opentelemetry-distributed-tracing-page) Follow requests through your distributed systems
  * [Databasee:](/docs/more-integrations/open-source-telemetry-integrations/opentelemetry/opentelemetry-view-your-data/opentelemetry-database-page) Monitor your database calls
  * [Errors:](/docs/more-integrations/open-source-telemetry-integrations/opentelemetry/opentelemetry-view-your-data/opentelemetry-errors-page)
  * [Errors inbox:](/docs/more-integrations/open-source-telemetry-integrations/opentelemetry/opentelemetry-view-your-data/opentelemetry-errors-inbox-page) See how to triage error groups
  * [External services:](/docs/more-integrations/open-source-telemetry-integrations/opentelemetry/opentelemetry-view-your-data/opentelemetry-external-services-page) Understand who is calling your application
  * [JVMs:](/docs/more-integrations/open-source-telemetry-integrations/opentelemetry/opentelemetry-view-your-data/opentelemetry-jvms-page) Monitor your JVM performance
  * [Logs:](/docs/more-integrations/open-source-telemetry-integrations/opentelemetry/opentelemetry-view-your-data/opentelemetry-logs-page) Gain insights from your logs
  * [Metrics explorer:](/docs/more-integrations/open-source-telemetry-integrations/opentelemetry/opentelemetry-view-your-data/opentelemetry-metrics-explorer-page) Look at specific metrics
  * [Transactions:](/docs/more-integrations/open-source-telemetry-integrations/opentelemetry/opentelemetry-view-your-data/opentelemetry-transactions-page) Examine transactions
  * [Data explorer and query builder:](/docs/more-integrations/open-source-telemetry-integrations/opentelemetry/opentelemetry-view-your-data/opentelemetry-data-explorer-query-builder) Try out these do-it-yourself tools to drill into your data
