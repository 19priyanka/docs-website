## Add a chart to a widget [#add-chart]

If you have not already selected the chart you want to show from the New Relic App, then follow these steps: 

1. Navigate to whatever chart you would like to see in the widget.
2. Tap on the chart to show the detail view.
3. Tap the **more** icon in the top right corner.
<br /><img style={{ width: '117px', height: '253px'}} title="icon-ios-add-to-widgets" alt="icon-ios-add-to-widgets" src={iosWidgetsInApp1}/>
4. Select **Add** to widgets.
5. You can also remove and re-arrange widgets with **Manage widgets**.
<br /><img style={{ width: '117px', height: '253px'}} title="icon-ios-add-to-widgets" alt="icon-ios-add-to-widgets" src={iosWidgetsInApp2}/>

## Designate charts for widgets [#designate-charts]

To select which chart is shown for each of the iOS widgets: 

1. Press and hold on the widget until it jiggles.
2. Tap **Select chart**, or the chart name.
<br /><img style={{ width: '117px', height: '253px'}} title="icon-ios-add-to-widgets" alt="icon-ios-add-to-widgets" src={iosWidgets5}/>
3. You may also hide the title and/or legend from this screen.
