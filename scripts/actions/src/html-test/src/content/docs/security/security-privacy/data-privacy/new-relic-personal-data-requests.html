There may be times that you want to process personal data in New Relic products or services about your own customers. We have taken steps so that you can do so in accordance with applicable data protection laws, such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the EU.

Responsibilities

When you receive a data subject request from one of your own customers, you are the data controller and are responsible for all interactions with the data subject. New Relic is your data processor and will only communicate with you as our customer.

  • For a complete overview of what data New Relic records, what it's used for, and how long this data is stored, see the New Relic Services Data Privacy Notice.
  • For additional information to help enable your compliance with privacy laws when using our browser monitoring service, see our documentation about New Relic cookies used for browser monitoring.
  • For more information about our data privacy and security measures, see our data privacy documentation, or visit the New Relic security website.

Requests for data access [#submit-request]

For data access requests from one of your customers, you have the ability to:

  • Query all data New Relic collected on your behalf
  • Extract the relevant information
  • Provide it to the data subject

Requests for data deletion

To improve the security of the data you entrust us with, we restrict the ability to request data deletion to only Admin and Owner roles. If you have not been assigned this role, please contact one of your account's Admins or Owners. To find your account's users, follow the instructions for viewing users.

When you want to delete data, an Admin or Owner can submit a ticket to support.newrelic.com to ask us for help. Please note that by law, we have 30 days to fulfill these requests once we receive them.

New Relic customers are responsible for:

  • Identifying the personal data that must be deleted
  • Determining how to dispose of the request
  • Providing New Relic with sufficient detail to complete the change you want
  • Responding to the original data subject request

Requests should come from authorized employees of direct customers of New Relic, meaning those individuals with an account that has access to New Relic products.

Submission process

I have a paid New Relic account

Please provide details of all enabled custom parameters that contain personal data that should be deleted or anonymized and open a ticket through support.newrelic.com under accounts.

I have a free New Relic account

New Relic can delete all data in your account in response to a request submitted to support.newrelic.com under accounts.

I'm not sure what type of account I have

To check your subscription:

  1. Go to rpm.newrelic.com.
  2. Click the account dropdown.
  3. Select Account settings > Account > Subscription.

I do not have a New Relic account

If you are not a customer of New Relic, please refer to our General Data Privacy Notice.
