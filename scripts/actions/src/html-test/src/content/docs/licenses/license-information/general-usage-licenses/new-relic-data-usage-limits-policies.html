New Relic places limits on the amount and rate of data that accounts can store and report. This resource lists the default account-level data usage limits.

Why it matters [#why_matters]

We understand that your configuration or other errors may cause your account to exceed these limits. But when this happens, your account, or any of your user accounts or sub-accounts, may experience one or both of the following:

  • Sampling of data
  • Temporary pause or cessation of data collection

To prevent data usage spikes from impacting other accounts, New Relic reserves the right to enact these policies. If you have questions about these default limits or your contract, contact your New Relic account representative.

Trace data usage limits [#traces]

Additional data usage limits apply to specific products. For more information, see the product section in this document.

Traces

Limit

Spans saved per minute sent to the Trace API. Applies to the account family.

The default span per minute rate limit is 3x the monthly purchased span amount and is calculated as:

(number of monthly purchased spans / 750 hours / 60 minutes) * 3
Up to a maximum of 2M spans per minute per account family.
